I hold a Ph.D. in Economics and Statistics from the University of Milano-Bicocca and have policy & research experience at the European Central Bank and the European Banking Authority.

My work combines structural macroeconomic modelling and empirical macroeconometrics to study firm dynamics, macro-financial linkages, and macroprudential policy.

**Research interests**: monetary policy, macroeconomics, macro-finance, financial intermediation, financial stability, applied econometrics, and Bayesian methods.
