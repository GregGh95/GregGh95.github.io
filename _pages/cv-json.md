## Publications

* **Residential real estate (RRE) lending standards: determinants and financial stability implications** (2025), with Giorgia De Nora, Ettore Durante, Alessandro Fontana, Matteo Forletta, Barbara Jarmulska, Cristina Perales, and Valentina Scalone.  
ECB Macroprudential Bulletin, No. 29.  
[Article](https://www.ecb.europa.eu/press/financial-stability-publications/macroprudential-bulletin/html/ecb.mpbu202506_01.it.html)  
This article studies the determinants of residential real estate lending standards and their financial stability implications. It highlights the procyclicality of lending standards and the way loose standards can amplify adverse housing market shocks.

## Working Papers

* **Endogenous Firms' Exit and the Equity Premium** (2025), Job Market Paper.  
[PDF](/files/JMP.pdf)  
DSGE model with endogenous firm entry and exit, combined with a Bayesian VAR, to study how macroeconomic shocks shape cyclical fluctuations in risk premia.

* **Determinants of House Prices in the Euro Area** (2025), with [Giorgia De Nora (ECB)](https://www.ecb.europa.eu/pub/research/authors/profiles/giorgia-de-nora.en.html).  
[PDF](/files/ECB_paper.pdf)  
Panel Bayesian VAR for eight euro area countries identifying monetary policy, mortgage supply, housing preference, and housing supply shocks, with implications for monetary and macroprudential policy design.

* **Bank Risk Weights, Credit Spillovers, and Macroeconomic Implications** (2026), with Olli Castren (EBA) and Raphael Kopp (EBA).  
DSGE-based analysis of risk weights shocks on bank lending and macroeconomic activity, with cross-country and bank-size heterogeneity in the euro area.
