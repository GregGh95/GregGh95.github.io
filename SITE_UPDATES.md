# Updating This Website

The live website is published at:

https://greggh95.github.io/

Most routine updates happen in the `_pages` folder:

- `_pages/about.md` controls the homepage.
- `_pages/research.md` controls the Research & Policy page at `/research/`.
- `_pages/teaching.md` controls the Teaching page at `/teaching/`.
- `_pages/cv.md` controls the CV page at `/cv/`.

PDFs and other downloadable files live in `files/`.

The top navigation is controlled by `_data/navigation.yml`.

After editing, commit and push to `master`. GitHub Pages will rebuild and publish the site automatically.
